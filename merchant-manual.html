Here’s a manual of your Connected Analytics
Merchant Dashboard

Total Visits:

Shows the total customer visits to all your registered stores within specified date range.

Purchase Amount:

This shows your total sales from card, cash and redeem transactions for specified date range.

Total Sale

This shows your total sales from card, cash and redeem transactions for specified date range

TUC Rewards:

Total rewards credited to your customers within specified date range.

TUC Plus Rewards:

Total ThankUCash plus rewards credited to your customers within specified date range.

TUC Plus Claimed Reward:

Amount redeemable by your customers on ThankUCash plus reward scheme.

Convenience Charge:

Total agreed ThankUCash commision per reward.

Total Redeem:

Total amount redeemed by your customers within specified date range.

Reward Percentage:

Reward percentage you give to your customers per transaction, inclusive of ThankUCash charge per reward.

Total Store Visits:

Total number of your stores customer visits within specified date range.

Sale Sources:

Percentage Breakdown of your Cash and Card sales within specified date range.

Card Sale Sources:

Card type used at point of purchase in your store.

Customers Store Visit Timings:

Graphical display of real time traffic information of your customers.

Customer Overview:

Total New, Referred, Unique and Repeating Customers as well as Customer description by Gender, Age Group and Purchase by Age Group.

Bank Collections:

Total money to be collected across all your outlets,from the bank, for transactions that went through ThankUCash.

Terminals:

All active and idle POS terminals in your store.

Recent Payments:

Latest real-time customer purchases in your store.
